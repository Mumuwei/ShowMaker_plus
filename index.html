ShowMaker++: A Unified Framework for High-Fidelity 2D Human Video Generation and Protection via Fine-Grained Diffusion Modeling and Pose Priors

Quanwei Yang1, Lingyun Yu1, Hongtao Xie1*
1University of Science and Technology of China

The overview of our proposed framework ShowMaker++.

Abstract

Despite recent advances in human video generation, existing works typically target either facial animation or full-body motion, making them inadequate for realistic conversational videos that require various facial expressions and frequent hand gestures. Moreover, most current approaches treat content generation and protection as separate problems, leaving generated videos and models vulnerable to misuse and potentially leading to broader societal concerns.

To address these limitations, we propose ShowMaker++, a unified 2D human video generation and protection framework that jointly produces high-fidelity half-body conversational videos and embeds interpretable protection signals within the same diffusion process. We adopt a dual-stream diffusion architecture as the backbone and further introduce two specialized modules for crucial local regions (i.e., hands and face), along with a seamlessly pluggable watermark injection module. Specifically, to handle the challenging hand generation caused by sparse motion guidance, we propose a Key Point-based Fine-grained Hand Modeling module by amplifying positional information from raw hand key points and constructing a corresponding key point-based codebook. To further enhance facial fidelity, we introduce a Face Recapture module, which extracts facial texture features and global identity features from the aligned face and integrates them into the diffusion process for face enhancement. Crucially, we embed a pose-prior–guided interpretable watermark directly into the diffusion process, enabling the model to jointly achieve high-quality video generation and built-in protection for both the generated content and the model itself.

Extensive quantitative and qualitative experiments demonstrate the superior visual quality, temporal consistency, and interpretable watermark verification capability of our method.
